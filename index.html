The Institute for Coherence Research is a cross-disciplinary initiative focused on understanding and engineering the persistence of order in complex systems.

From foundational physics to biological networks, we explore how feedback fields—like the Coherence-Regulating Field—can shape, stabilize, and extend coherent structures in a world driven by noise and decay.
