Research: The Coherence-Regulating Field

The Coherence-Regulating Field (CRF) is a theoretical framework that introduces a universal feedback field capable of preserving coherent structures in noisy or diffusive systems.

CRF proposes that in regions of high structural order, an auxiliary field H(x,t) senses that order and dynamically reduces its rate of decay. This results in longer-lasting patterns—whether physical, chemical, or biological.
